## 2) Generate Reports (CSV/Excel)

**Navigate to:** `Reports`

Available report categories include:

- Student data
- Staff info and attendance data
- Billing and invoices
- Activity logs
- Form submission data

### How to generate a report

1. Select the report type.
2. Choose a date range and relevant filters.
3. Click **Generate Report**.
4. Download the CSV from the right panel once ready.

> Reports are exportable in spreadsheet format for audits, management sharing, and third-party uploads.
