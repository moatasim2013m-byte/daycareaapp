This guide consolidates parent-facing onboarding and bus tracking instructions for Illumine.

## Getting Started with Illumine for Parents

### 1) Download the app

- **Android phones/tablets:** search for **Illumine** on Google Play, or open:
  - https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.illumine.app
- **Apple devices (iPhone/iPad):** search for **Illumine for Parents** on the App Store, or open:
  - https://apps.apple.com/in/app/illumine-for-parents/id1459249394

### 2) Log in to Illumine

Registered parents can log in using either their phone number with OTP or email/password.

#### Option A: Phone number + OTP

1. Tap **Login to Illumine**.
2. Choose **Log in with Phone number**.
3. Select the correct country and enter the phone number registered with the school (without `0` prefix or country code prefix).
4. Enter the One Time Password (OTP) sent to your number.

#### Option B: Email + password

1. Tap **Login to Illumine**.
2. Choose **Login with Email**.
3. Enter your registered email address.
4. Enter the password from your invitation email.
   - If the invitation email is unavailable, use **Reset password** to get a new password.

## Bus Tracking with Illumine

Illumine allows parents and administrators to track school buses and access driver contact details.

### Teacher/staff setup (route creation)

1. In the teacher app, go to **More ‚Üí Bus Tracking**.
2. Tap **New Route**.
3. Enter route details:
   - Route name
   - Journey type (pickup/drop-off)
   - Location
   - Vehicle details and vehicle number
4. Assign a **driver** and optional **helper**.
5. Select students for the route.
6. Add student addresses and drop-off times.
7. Tap **Save**.

### Add drivers (web portal)

1. Go to **Management ‚Üí Staff**.
2. Click **Add teacher**.
3. Fill in name, gender, address, phone number, and email.
4. Assign role as **Driver**, choose classrooms, then submit.
5. Driver can log in with registered phone or email.
6. Driver will only see their assigned route page.

### Driver trip workflow

1. Open the assigned route and tap **Drive ‚Üí Start trip**.
2. Mark each child as:
   - **Picked up**, or
   - **Absent**.
3. Use filters to review picked-up/pending/absent students.
4. Stop trip when all pickups for that segment are complete.
5. Repeat the same process for drop-off.

### Parent view (live tracking)

1. Tap **Profile** in the app footer.
2. Select **Bus Tracking**.
3. Choose route using **Select route**.
4. View:
   - Live GPS location
   - Driver profile and contact details
   - Estimated arrival time
5. Tap the **Call** action next to the driver/helper profile to contact them.
