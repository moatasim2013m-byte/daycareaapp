# Student Attendance Guide

## Access Student Attendance
1. From the left-hand menu, go to **Attendance & Leaves**.
2. Click **Student Attendance**.
3. You will land on the attendance dashboard for the selected date.

## Understand the Attendance Dashboard
At the top of the page, you can view a quick summary:

- **All Students** – Total students for the selected filters
- **Pending** – Students whose attendance has not been marked yet
- **Check In** – Students currently checked in
- **Check Out** – Students who have been checked out
- **Absent** – Students marked absent

This gives you a quick overview of attendance for the day.

## Filter Attendance View
Use the filters at the top to narrow down the list:

- **Date** – Select the day you want to record or view attendance
- **Rooms** – Filter by classroom
- **Groups** – Filter by group
- **Programs** – Filter by program

This is useful when managing attendance for specific classes.

## Mark Student Attendance
You can mark attendance directly from the student list.

### Check In a Student
1. Find the student in the list.
2. Click **Check In** next to their name.
3. The check-in time is recorded automatically.

### Check Out a Student
1. Click **Check Out** next to the student’s name.
2. The check-out time is saved.

### Mark a Student Absent
1. Select the student.
2. Click **Mark Absent**.

This helps maintain accurate daily records.

## Mark Attendance in Bulk
To mark attendance for multiple students:

1. Select students using the checkboxes.
2. Choose an action from the top toolbar:
   - **Check In**
   - **Check Out**
   - **Mark Absent**

This saves time during busy check-in hours.

## Daily and Monthly View
You can switch between:

- **Daily view** – For marking attendance for a specific day
- **Monthly view** – To review attendance trends and records

## Search for a Student
Use the search bar to quickly locate a student and update their attendance without scrolling through the full list.

## Why This Matters
Maintaining accurate attendance helps your center:

- Track student presence in real time
- Maintain safety records
- Share updates with parents
- Generate attendance reports
- Monitor absence trends
