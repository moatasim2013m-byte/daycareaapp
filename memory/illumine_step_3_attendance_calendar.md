## 3) Set Up School Calendar

The School Calendar helps manage and share school-wide events, holidays, and important dates with staff and families.

### Navigate to Calendar

1. Log in to your Illumine dashboard.
2. From the left-hand menu, click **Calendar**.
3. Under Calendar, use:
   - **Staff Schedule**: View/manage staff schedules
   - **Student Schedule**: View/manage student schedules
   - **Staff Holidays**: Manage staff leave/holiday calendars
   - **Birthday Calendar**: Track student and staff birthdays
   - **School Calendar**: Manage school-wide events, holidays, important dates
4. Click **School Calendar**.

### School Calendar - Monthly View

From monthly view, you can:

- View all events, holidays, and important dates
- Switch between months
- Search events by name
- Filter by rooms, category (Event/Holiday/Important Date), and time range
- Mark/view **Staff Only** events
- Use **Create** to add an event
- Use **Bulk upload** to add multiple events

### Create a School Calendar Entry

1. Go to `Calendar â†’ School Calendar`.
2. Click **Create** (top-right).
3. Fill required details:
   - Event Name
   - Type: Event, Holiday, or Important Date
   - Start Date and Time
   - End Date and Time
   - Description (optional)
   - Rooms (optional scope to classrooms)
   - **Staff Only** (if applicable)
4. Click **Post/Save**.

The event appears in the School Calendar.

### Bulk Upload School Calendar

1. Go to `Calendar â†’ School Calendar`.
2. Click **Bulk upload**.
3. Click **Download Format** (CSV template).
4. Fill event details in CSV.
5. Upload completed CSV.

Important CSV rules:

- Do not remove the header row
- Date format: `DD-MM-YYYY`
- Time format: `HH:MM`
- Event type must be one of:
  - `Holiday`
  - `Event`
  - `Important Date`

After upload, entries are added to School Calendar and can be edited individually.
