# Bus Tracking With Illumine

**Written by:** Varun  
**Last updated:** Over a year ago

Illumine allows parents and administrators to track school buses directly from the app. It also gives access to driver contact details to improve child safety and route transparency.

## Set up bus tracking routes (Teacher App)

1. Open the **Teacher App**.
2. Go to **More** → **Bus Tracking**.
3. Click **New Route**.
4. Enter route details:
   - Route name
   - Journey type (**Pick-up** or **Drop-off**)
   - Location
   - Vehicle details and number
5. Select the **Driver** and optional **Helper**.
6. Select students to assign to the route.
7. Add each student’s address and drop-off time.
8. Click **Save**.

Once the bus starts the journey, parents can track it from the **Bus Tracking** option in their profile.

## Add drivers in Illumine (Web Portal)

1. Open the **Web Portal**.
2. Go to **Management** → **Staff**.
3. Click **Add Teacher**.
4. Enter staff details:
   - Name
   - Gender
   - Address
   - Phone number
   - Email ID
5. Assign role as **Driver**.
6. Choose classrooms.
7. Click **Submit**.

The driver is now added and can log in using their registered phone number or email ID. Drivers can only view routes assigned to them.

## Driver trip flow

1. Driver logs in and opens **Drive**.
2. Click **Start Trip**.
3. Review student list and mark status:
   - **Picked up** for onboarded students
   - **Absent** for no-show students
4. Use filters to view **Picked Up**, **Pending**, or **Absent** students.
5. After completing all pickups, click **Stop Trip**.
6. Repeat the same flow for drop-offs.

Admins can monitor the same trip information through their bus tracking interface.

## Parent view (Live tracking)

Parents receive a notification once their child is onboarded.

1. Open the app and tap **Profile**.
2. Tap **Bus Tracking**.
3. Select a route from **Select Route**.
4. View:
   - Live GPS location
   - Driver profile
   - Driver contact details
   - Estimated arrival time
5. Tap the **Call** button beside the driver profile to contact the driver/helper.

## Summary

Illumine’s bus tracking helps schools and parents stay coordinated during pick-up and drop-off with real-time visibility and direct contact options.
