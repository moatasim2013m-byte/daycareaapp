Onboarding students and adding parent/guardian details in illumine is designed to be quick and straightforward. Keeping parent contact details current is important for key communications such as invoices, receipts, announcements, newsletters, and onboarding invites.

## How to Add a Student

1. From the left-side menu, open **Center Management**.
2. Click **Students**.
3. In the student list view, click **Add Student** (top-right).
4. Fill in the required fields in the **Add Student** form:
   - Student Name
   - Preferred Name (optional)
   - Gender
   - Room (classroom assignment)
   - Status (e.g., Active)
5. Click **Save**.

## How to Add Parent Details

1. From the left-side menu, open **Center Management**.
2. Click **Students**.
3. Select the student profile you want to update.
4. Scroll to **Parent Details**.
5. Click **Father** or **Mother** (as applicable).
6. Enter the required details:
   - Name
   - Phone number
   - Email address
7. Click **Submit**.

## What Happens Next

- Parent details are saved to the system.
- The details are used for communication, notifications, and onboarding.
- Parent information can be updated at any time.
