# Staff Attendance

_Last updated: over 3 weeks ago_

The **Staff Attendance** section allows you to record and monitor staff check-ins, check-outs, and absences for each day. From a single dashboard, you can quickly update attendance, view staff availability, and ensure smooth daily operations.

This helps your center maintain accurate staff records and stay organized throughout the day.

## Access Staff Attendance

1. From the left-hand menu, go to **Attendance & Leaves**.
2. Click **Staff Attendance**.
3. You will land on the staff attendance dashboard for the selected date.

## Understand the Dashboard

At the top of the page, you’ll see a quick summary:

- **All Staffs** – Total staff for the selected filters
- **Pending** – Staff whose attendance hasn’t been marked yet
- **Check In** – Staff currently checked in
- **Check Out** – Staff who have checked out
- **Absent** – Staff marked absent

This overview helps you quickly understand attendance status for the day.

## Filter the Attendance View

Use the filters at the top to view specific staff:

- **Date** – Select the day
- **Rooms** – Filter by classroom
- **Roles** – Filter by staff role

This makes it easy to manage attendance for specific teams or classrooms.

## Mark Staff Attendance

You can update attendance directly from the list.

### Check In Staff

1. Locate the staff member.
2. Click **Check In** next to their name.
3. The check-in time will be recorded automatically.

### Check Out Staff

1. Click **Check Out** next to the staff member’s name.
2. The system will record their check-out time.

### Mark Staff Absent

1. Select the staff member.
2. Click **Mark Absent**.
3. This ensures accurate daily records.

## Mark Attendance in Bulk

To update multiple staff members at once:

1. Select staff using the checkboxes.
2. Click one of the options:
   - **Check In**
   - **Check Out**
   - **Mark Absent**

This is helpful during busy start-of-day or end-of-day times.

## Daily and Monthly View

You can switch between:

- **Daily view** – For marking attendance on a specific day
- **Monthly view** – To review attendance records over time

## Search for Staff

Use the search bar to quickly find a staff member and update their attendance without scrolling through the full list.

## Why This Matters

Tracking staff attendance helps your center:

- Ensure proper classroom coverage
- Maintain compliance and records
- Monitor staffing patterns
- Plan shifts effectively
