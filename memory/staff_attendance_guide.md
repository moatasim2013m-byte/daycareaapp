# Staff Attendance (Admin Guide)

The **Staff Attendance** section allows center admins to record and monitor staff check-ins, check-outs, and absences for each day. From a single dashboard, teams can quickly update attendance, view staff availability, and keep daily operations organized.

## Access Staff Attendance
1. Open the left-hand menu and go to **Attendance & Leaves**.
2. Click **Staff Attendance**.
3. The staff attendance dashboard opens for the selected date.

## Dashboard Overview
At the top of the page, the dashboard shows:
- **All Staffs**: Total staff count for active filters.
- **Pending**: Staff whose attendance has not yet been marked.
- **Check In**: Staff currently checked in.
- **Check Out**: Staff already checked out.
- **Absent**: Staff marked absent.

This gives a quick daily attendance snapshot.

## Filter the Attendance View
Use the top filters to narrow results:
- **Date**: Select the day.
- **Rooms**: Filter by classroom.
- **Roles**: Filter by staff role.

## Mark Staff Attendance
Attendance can be updated directly from the list.

### Check In Staff
1. Locate the staff member.
2. Click **Check In** next to their name.
3. The system records check-in time automatically.

### Check Out Staff
1. Locate the staff member.
2. Click **Check Out**.
3. The system records check-out time automatically.

### Mark Staff Absent
1. Select the staff member.
2. Click **Mark Absent**.

## Mark Attendance in Bulk
To update multiple staff members at once:
1. Select staff using checkboxes.
2. Choose one bulk action:
   - **Check In**
   - **Check Out**
   - **Mark Absent**

This is useful during busy start-of-day and end-of-day periods.

## Daily and Monthly Views
- **Daily view**: Mark attendance for a specific day.
- **Monthly view**: Review attendance records over time.

## Search for Staff
Use the search bar to quickly find a staff member and update attendance without scrolling through the full list.

## Why It Matters
Tracking staff attendance helps centers:
- Ensure proper classroom coverage.
- Maintain compliance and records.
- Monitor staffing patterns.
- Plan shifts effectively.
